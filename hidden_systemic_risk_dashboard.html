Fragility Convergence

All 5 risks share a common feature: they're invisible until they're not. Passive flows, private credit, CRE marks, Treasury liquidity, and geopolitical tail risks all appear "fine" until stress reveals the hidden fragility. Traditional risk dashboards miss them because they're not in the daily data.

The Transmission Mechanism

A shock in any one area can cascade: Geopolitical event ‚Üí Treasury basis trade unwind ‚Üí 60/40 failure ‚Üí Passive flow reversal ‚Üí Private credit discovery ‚Üí Regional bank stress ‚Üí Credit contraction. The interconnections are the real risk ‚Äî not any single factor.
